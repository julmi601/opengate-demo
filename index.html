What we do

Tailored services for refined living

🌿
Property & Gardens

Lawn care, hedging, seasonal planting, gutters, exterior painting, pool servicing — every aspect of your property kept to the standard you expect, without you having to ask twice.

🔧
Household & Repairs

Plumbing, electrical, handyman, appliance installation, safety modifications. A fully vetted network of tradespeople who know your home — and who you can trust inside it.

👤
Wellbeing & Care

Regular check-ins from your Home Manager, proactive safety assessments, coordination of personal care, appointments, transport, companionship, and meal delivery.

📋
Family Visibility

A shared dashboard gives your family real-time insight — who's visiting, what's been done, how you're doing. Complete transparency, complete peace of mind from any distance.

🚗
Errands & Logistics

Grocery shopping, prescription collection, appointment transport, bill management. The errands that pile up are quietly handled — so you can focus on enjoying your day.

🆘
24/7 Emergency Cover

One number, any time. Whether it's a burst pipe at midnight or an urgent health concern, your Home Manager coordinates the response — calmly, reliably, immediately.

Our promise

A sanctuary for your independent future

Open Gate provides bespoke property and household management for Auckland's most discerning seniors. Our mission is to transform the complexity of home maintenance into a seamless, invisible support system — allowing you to age in place with the dignity, grace, and independence you deserve.

Every client is assigned a dedicated Home Manager who becomes a trusted constant in your life. One person who knows your home, your preferences, and your needs completely.
